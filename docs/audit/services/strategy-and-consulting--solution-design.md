### TechD Solution Design Process

If you are seeking help finding the issue, we can start with an assessment of your environment. We offer both on-site and remote assessments to gauge how your environments are performing. We look for the issues we can eliminate to optimize the performance of your software and hardware through troubleshooting and best practices.

If you have a good idea of the issue and are looking for a specific type of solution, we are happy to schedule a demo with you and your team, based on your needs and what you would like to see.

If you are having issues with non-IBM software that can be resolved through \_\_\_\_\_ (architecture? Resource allocation?) then we are happy to service your environment.
